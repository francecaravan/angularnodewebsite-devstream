Plot 537 - Canaberies


Welcome to the website page for the Mills family caravan. The caravan is located at plot 537 on the Canaberies site just north of Sainte Maxime. The plot has been in the Mills family since 2002. A new caravan has been placed on the site. Other attractions include a fresh water swimming pool. The Canaberies site is 5 minutes from the auto-route, 10 minutes from Le Muy and 20 minutes from Saint Maxime.
